## What's New in Your Admin Dashboard

You now have a complete **Courier Management System** with the following enhancements:

---

### New Quick Action Buttons
Located at the top right of the Admin Dashboard:

The dashboard now shows a detailed table with:

## 🎯 Key Features

### 1. **Courier Management Page** (`/admin/couriers`)
**What you can do:**
- ✅ View all couriers with their status
- ✅ Search by name, email, or phone number
- ✅ See performance metrics at a glance
- ✅ View, Edit, or Deactivate any courier
- ✅ Reactivate inactive couriers

**Quick Stats:**
- Total couriers count
- Active couriers count
- Inactive couriers count

### 2. **Courier Details Page** 
**Access:** Click the eye icon (👁️) next to any courier

**View:**
- Complete courier profile
- Statistics cards:
  - Total parcels assigned
  - Delivered count
  - In transit
  - Pending
  - Cancelled
  - Delivery rate (with visual bar)
- **⭐ Customer Rating** - Average stars and review count
- 📊 Avg delivery time in days
- 💰 Total revenue generated
- 📦 Last 20 parcels assigned
- 💬 Last 10 customer reviews

### 3. **Manage Courier Information**
**Access:** Click pencil icon (✏️) on any courier

**Edit:**
- Full name
- Email address
- Phone number

### 4. **Deactivate/Reactivate Couriers**
**Buttons:** Lock icon (🔒) to deactivate, Unlock icon (🔓) to activate

**Why deactivate?**
- Remove underperforming couriers
- Temporary suspension without deleting data
- All courier information is preserved
- Can be reactivated anytime

### 5. **Performance Report** 
**Access:** Dashboard → Click "Performance" button

**View:**
- Performance leaderboard (sorted by rating & delivery rate)
- All courier stats in one table:
  - Rank/position
  - Performance metrics
  - Delivery rates
  - Customer ratings
  - Revenue generated
- Top 3 performers in each category:
  - Top Rated couriers
  - Highest Delivery Rate
  - Top Revenue earners

### 6. **Ratings & Reviews Report**
**Access:** Dashboard → Click "Ratings" button

**Features:**
- 🔍 **Filter by:**
  - Specific courier
  - Minimum star rating (1-5 stars)
  
- 📊 **Sort by:**
  - Newest first
  - Oldest first
  - Highest rating
  - Lowest rating

- **View:**
  - Full customer reviews
  - Star ratings (visual)
  - Customer name
  - Related parcel info
  - Review date & time

**Summary Stats:**
- Total ratings received
- Average rating across all filtered results
- Count of 5-star reviews
- Count of 1-2 star reviews

## 📈 Sample Workflow

### Monitor Courier Performance:
1. Go to **Admin Dashboard**
2. Look at the **Courier Performance Table** 
   - Sort by rating and see top performers
   - Identify couriers with low ratings
3. Click **"Ratings"** button to read specific reviews
4. If ratings are low:
   - Click **"Manage Couriers"**
   - Click **View** (👁️) on the courier
   - Check their recent parcels and reviews
   - Click **Edit** (✏️) to update contact info
   - Click **Deactivate** (🔒) if needed

### Generate Performance Reports:
1. Click **"Performance"** button
2. Review the leaderboard
3. Check top performers and underperformers
4. Identify trends and patterns
5. Use data for courier performance reviews or bonuses

## 💡 Tips

1. **Quick Search:** On the Courier Management page, use the search box to quickly find a specific courier by name, email, or phone

2. **Performance Tracking:** Check the Performance Report weekly to identify top performers and those needing support

3. **Quality Control:** Regularly review the Ratings Report to catch any quality issues early

4. **Data Safety:** When deactivating a courier, all their data is preserved. You can reactivate them anytime

5. **Audit Trail:** All admin actions (deactivations, edits) are logged for compliance and tracking
